Ceremony

Seacoast Dr & Descanso Ave, Imperial Beach, CA 91932

The ceremony will start promptly at 2PM, so please leave for the site by 1PM to allow for traffic and parking.If possible, you may want to meet at the Reception house and Uber or carpool down to the beach. It is about a 25-minute drive with NO traffic from the Reception site. It is the weekend before 4th of July, so beach parking may be hard to find.

Directions

  1. Take I-5 South to Coronado Ave
  2. Turn Right on Coronado Ave towards the ocean. Coronado Ave will become Imperial Beach Blvd
  3. Continue down Imperial Beach Blvd all the way to the end (Seacoast Dr)
  4. Turn Left on Seacoast Dr
  5. Continue down Seacoast Dr (1400 landmark address) to Descanso Ave and turn Right to drop off guests
  6. The set-up will be at the end of the street towards the ocean on the left or right

Parking

  • The landmark address is 1400 Seacoast Dr and Descanso Ave.
  • There is a small parking lot on Descanso Ave, however they ask that only the Bride and Groom use this lot.
  • Guests who are driving MUST drop guests off and then park in public parking lot at Seacoast Dr and Elkwood. Parking is $2.00 and it’s about a 10 min walk back to Descanso.
  • Parking can sometimes be found on the street, but it’s not reliable.
